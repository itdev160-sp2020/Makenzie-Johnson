List of My Favorite Animals

Snow Leopard

My favorite animal is the snow leopard. Snow leopards are one of the five big cats that live in Central Asia. They were originally thought to be related to the leopard due to their similar appearances. However, recent research has proved that this is not correct. Snow leopards have gray and white fur with black spots and a striped tail. They are known for their large paws, which helps them walk on the snow easier.

Fun Fact

  • Snow Leopards cannot roar because their small throats is similar to a cat's. However, they can make other noises such hissing, growling, moaning, and yowling.

Tiger

My second favorite animal is the tiger, which is the largest big cat in the world. Depending on their geographical location, tigers can be found in a variety of habitats. This ranges from tropical forests, grasslands, savannas, and even mangrove swamps. Tigers have reddish-orange fur with prominent black stripes.

Fun Fact

  • Tigers are adept swimmers and can easily cross a rivers and lakes 5 miles wide.
